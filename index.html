Go to see Maiko in Kyoto!

"Maiko" and "Geiko" are female performers who perform music and dance on a stage or at a benquet. Maiko are apprentices usually between 15 and 20 years old who become geiko when they finish their trainning. In Kyoto, there are five kagai(geisha district).They live in okiya(teahouse which maiko and geiko belong to) to learn tranditional customs and performances.
